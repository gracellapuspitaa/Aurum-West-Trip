Chronicles of Courage: The History of Surabaya

Image Statue

The story of Surabaya is a timeless chronicle of courage against the odds. Long before it earned its moniker as the "City of Heroes" during the monumental clash of 1945, Surabaya's destiny was always intertwined with bravery dating back to 1293 when local forces repelled the formidable Mongol invaders. Explore deep-seated history, the revolutionary echoes of Bung Tomo's speeches, and the historic landmarks that stand as monuments to freedom. Discover how the past continues to inspire the future in the heart of East Java.
